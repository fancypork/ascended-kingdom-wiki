Money From Mobs

🪙 Earning Money by Fighting

Killing mobs automatically rewards you with in-game currency. Different mobs pay out different amounts — tougher mobs like Blazes, Withers, and Elder Guardians earn significantly more than basic overworld mobs. Keep an eye on the chat after a kill to see your payout.

Tip: Pair mob grinding with mcMMO's Combat skills to double-dip on EXP and money gains at the same time.
Plugin Guide
Towny

Towny lets players form towns, buy and sell land plots, create governments, and band together into nations. It's the foundation of community life on Ascended Kingdom.

📜 How It Works

The world is divided into chunks. Towns claim chunks as plots, and residents can be granted access to those plots. Towns need an active Mayor, regular upkeep payments, and enough residents to grow into a thriving nation.

New player tip: You can join an existing town for free using /t join [townname] if the town has open registration, or ask a Mayor to invite you with /t add [yourname].

Basic Commands

CommandDescription
/tView info about your current town.
/t [name]View info about any town by name.
/t listList all towns on the server.
/t join [town]Join a town with open registration.
/t leaveLeave your current town.
/t spawn [town]Teleport to a town's spawn point.
/t mapShow a text map of claimed chunks near you.
/resident or /resView your own resident info and town membership.
Towny
Creating & Managing Towns

Ready to lead? Any player can found a town — it costs money to create and requires ongoing upkeep to keep your claims active.

Founding a Town

CommandDescription
/t new [name]Found a new town at your current location. Costs money.
/t claimClaim the chunk you're standing in as town land.
/t unclaimUnclaim a chunk from your town.
/t add [player]Invite a player to your town (Mayor only).
/t kick [player]Remove a resident from your town.

⚠️ Upkeep: Towns are charged a daily upkeep fee from the town bank. If the bank runs dry, the town will fall and its claims will be released. Keep your bank funded!

Plots & Permissions

Plots are individual chunks within a town. Mayors can sell, assign, or restrict plots — and players can customize who can do what on their own plots.

Plot Commands

CommandDescription
/plot claimClaim an available plot you're standing in (costs money if for sale).
/plot unclaimRelease a plot you own back to the town.
/plot forsale [price]Put your plot up for sale at a set price.
/plot notforsaleRemove your plot from sale.
/plot set name [name]Give your plot a custom name.
/plot set perm [on/off]Toggle build/destroy/switch/item permissions on your plot for outsiders, residents, or allies.
/plot toggle pvpToggle PvP on your specific plot.
Plot types include standard, shop, arena, embassy, and more. Mayors can set a plot's type with /plot set [type] to unlock special behaviors for that chunk.

mcMMO

mcMMO adds an RPG-style leveling system to Minecraft. Every action you take in-game — mining, fighting, fishing, farming — earns XP in a related skill, which unlocks passive bonuses and active abilities.

📈 Leveling Up

XP is earned automatically as you play. There's no special setup needed — just do what you normally do. As your skill levels rise, you'll unlock better passive bonuses and more powerful active abilities.

Key Commands

CommandDescription
/mcstatsView all your skill levels at a glance.
/[skillname]View detailed info about a specific skill (e.g. /mining, /swords).
/mctopView the server leaderboard for total mcMMO power level.
/mctop [skill]View the leaderboard for a specific skill.
/mcrankSee your own ranking on the server leaderboards.
/party create [name]Create an mcMMO party to share XP and abilities with friends.
Active abilities: Most combat and gathering skills have an activatable ability. Hold the relevant tool and right-click to ready the ability, then use it on the target.

Money & Server Economy

Ascended Kingdom has a player-driven economy. Earn money by fighting mobs, selling items, and running shops — then spend it on land, gear, and more.

Earning Money

🪙 Money From Mobs

Kill mobs to earn automatic cash payouts. Harder mobs pay more. Bosses and nether/end mobs are especially lucrative.

🏪 Selling at Shops

Set up your own EzShop or sell items through PlayerAuctions and EzAuctions to earn money from other players.

🏙️ Towny Jobs

Mayors can pay residents from the town bank. Forming a productive town and contributing to it is another great income stream.

Money Commands

CommandDescription
/balance or /balCheck your current money.
/pay [player] [amount]Send money directly to another player.

EzAuctions

EzAuctions lets you auction items to the entire server in real-time. Other players can bid and the highest bid when the timer runs out wins.

How to Auction

Hold the item you want to auction in your hand before running the auction command.
CommandDescription
/auction start [price] [increment]Start an auction for the item in your hand at a starting price.
/auction bid [amount]Place a bid on the current auction.
/auction infoShow details about the item currently being auctioned.
/auction cancelCancel your active auction (if no bids have been placed).
/auction ignoreMute auction announcements in chat.
/auction queueView upcoming auctions in the queue.
Note: Once a bid has been placed on your auction, you cannot cancel it. Make sure your starting price is right before you begin!
Economy
EzShops

EzShops lets you create persistent player-owned shops where others can buy and sell items from you, even when you're offline.

Setting Up a Shop

1️⃣ Place a Chest

Place a chest where you want your shop to be. Stock it with whatever items you want to sell.

2️⃣ Create the Shop Sign

Place a sign on the chest and write the shop details. EzShops will format it into a shop automatically.

3️⃣ Open for Business

Other players can walk up to your shop, right-click the sign, and buy or sell items. You earn money even while offline.

Shop Commands

CommandDescription
/shop listBrowse a list of all active player shops.
/shop find [item]Search for a shop that sells a specific item.
/shop removeRemove a shop by clicking its sign.
Tip: Set up your shop inside your town for safety. Shops in unclaimed land can be griefed.

Player Auctions

PlayerAuctions is a browse-anytime marketplace where players list items for sale at a fixed price or as an auction. Unlike EzAuctions, listings persist after the auctioneer logs off.

Using the Marketplace

CommandDescription
/ahOpen the auction house GUI to browse all active listings.
/ah sell [price]List the item in your hand for sale at a fixed price.
/ah listView your own active listings.
/ah cancelCancel one of your active listings and get the item back.
/ah expiredCollect items from expired or unsold listings.
/ah search [term]Search the auction house for a specific item.
Tip: Check /ah regularly for deals. Rare enchanted gear and hard-to-farm resources often appear here at competitive prices.
Quality of Life
GravesX

When you die, GravesX spawns a grave at your location containing all your items. You won't lose your gear on death — as long as you can find your way back.

⚰️ How Graves Work

On death, a grave block appears at your location. Your items are safely stored inside. When you return and right-click the grave, your items are returned to your inventory automatically.

Commands

CommandDescription
/gravesView a list of all your active graves and their locations.
/graves teleportTeleport directly to your most recent grave (if enabled on server).
⚠️ Important: Graves do not last forever. After a set amount of time, the grave will break and drop the items on the ground. Retrieve your grave quickly after dying!
Tip: After dying in a dangerous area (nether, caves), prepare before going back — bring weapons, food, and armor so you don't die again reaching your grave.

Quality of Life
Silk Spawners

Silk Spawners lets you pick up mob spawners with a Silk Touch enchanted pickaxe and place them elsewhere. Build your own mob farms wherever you want!

📦 How to Pick Up a Spawner

Equip a pickaxe with the Silk Touch enchantment. Mine any mob spawner and it will drop as an item rather than breaking. You can then carry it and place it wherever you like.

Tip: Spawners are valuable! Consider using them in a custom mob farm in your town to earn Money From Mobs passively and level up mcMMO combat skills at the same time.
Note: You need a Silk Touch pickaxe — a regular pickaxe or non-enchanted tool will destroy the spawner. Always double-check before mining one!
Quality of Life
Rose Stacker

Rose Stacker improves server performance and your experience by stacking nearby mobs, items, and spawners into single entities. Instead of 50 individual zombies lagging the server, you see one "x50 Zombie" stack.

🌿 What Gets Stacked

Mobs of the same type that are near each other are merged into a single stacked entity with a count above their head. Items on the ground are also stacked. When you kill a stacked mob or pick up a stacked item, you get loot for the whole stack.

AoE abilities help: mcMMO abilities like Skull Splitter (Axes) and Serrated Strikes (Swords) that hit multiple targets are especially powerful against stacked mobs.
📦 Stacked Spawners

Spawners of the same type placed next to each other may stack, allowing a single spawner block to act as multiple spawners — great for compact, high-output mob farms.

Skoice – Proximity Voice Chat

Skoice connects Minecraft to Discord for in-game proximity voice chat. Players nearby hear you in-game through Discord — and the further away they are, the quieter you sound.

🎙️ How to Set Up

Skoice requires linking your Minecraft account to a Discord account via our server's Discord bot. Once linked, join the designated Skoice voice channel in Discord and you'll hear players around you automatically based on in-game proximity.

Commands

CommandDescription
/skoice linkGet a code to link your Minecraft account to Discord.
/skoice settingsAdjust your personal voice chat settings in-game.
/skoice unlinkUnlink your Minecraft account from Discord.
Tip: You don't have to talk to benefit from Skoice — you can listen to nearby players even if you're muted in Discord. Great for trading or coordinating in towns without typing.
Requirement: You must be in the correct Discord voice channel for Skoice to work. Check with server staff or the Discord for the right channel to join.
